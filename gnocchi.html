Creamy Green Gnocchi Bake

  • 1 smasll butternut squash
  • 2 sprigs rosemary
  • 1 x 400g canbutter beans
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 large handfuls kale
  • large handful parsley
  • handful of walnuts

Method



  1. Preheat oven to 220°C / 200°C fan / 425°F. Place the cashew and sunflower seeds in a small bowl and cover with the boiling water; set aside for 5 minutes. Meanwhile, bring a large saucepan of lightly salted water to the boil, add the gnocchi and simmer for 2 minutes to par-cook. Drain and set aside.
  2. Remove the tray from the oven and take off the foil. Carefully lift out the garlic cloves, then mix in the mushrooms. Return the tray to tTip the soaked cashews, sunflower seeds, their soaking liquid, garlic, spinach, oat milk, lemon juice and nutritional yeast into a blender and blitz to form a thick sauce. In a small casserole dish, stir the sauce through the gnocchi and add the frozen peas.
  3. For the topping, put the breadcrumbs, parsley, nutritional yeast, sunflower seeds and oil into a bowl with a pinch of salt and mix so that everything is coated. Sprinkle on top of the gnocchi and bake for 15–20 minutes until golden and simmering.
